Why we built SeniorConnex.

SeniorConnex began with the people we love. This is the story of the Hinkley family, and the promise behind everything we build, to keep seniors safe, connected, and living life on their terms.

The Hinkley family story.

More than technology. A promise.

We've each cared for someone we love and felt the worry of not knowing what's happening when we can't be there. SeniorConnex was built to answer that worry, to bring everyone together around the senior and create peace of mind for the whole family.

  • Bring families and caregivers into one shared picture
  • Put privacy and dignity first, always
