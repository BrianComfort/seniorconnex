The hardest part of caring for a parent is the hour you can't be there. SeniorConnex fills that gap with three simple promises.

You'll know right away

A fall or a worrying change in vitals reaches you by text in seconds, no waiting for a second-hand voicemail.

You can check anytime

Open the app at 2 a.m. and the answer is already there, resting comfortably, all clear.

Everyone stays in sync

Siblings, spouses, and caregivers all see the same picture, so no one's left out and nothing falls through the cracks.

The alert reaches you in seconds.

When something matters, SeniorConnex sends it straight to your phone, with a clear acknowledgment so the whole family knows it landed. Open it in any web browser, no app to install.

How it works

Sleep through the night again.

SeniorConnex is quiet 99% of the time, and crystal clear for the 1% that matters. You don't have to call to check in or wonder if today was a good day. The phone stays calm until it genuinely needs you.

  • Alerts only when something truly matters
  • Clear, plain-language updates, no medical jargon
  • Choose who gets paged first, and who hears it second
  • Privacy-first: no cameras where they don't belong
