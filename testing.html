Our purpose

Aging at home shouldn't mean aging alone.

We built SeniorConnex so every family can know — in real time — that the person they love is safe, well, and home. Not with a button to press. Not with a wristband to remember. With a device that simply takes care of the watching, so you can get back to the loving.

The Device

One device.
No wearables.
No charging.
No buttons.

SeniorConnex sits on a shelf or a side table and disappears into the room. Nothing to wear. Nothing to remember. Nothing to plug in nightly. It just works — quietly tracking heart rate, blood pressure, and location while the people you love live their lives the way they always have.

How it works

Three things, watched continuously, without anyone noticing.

01 — Vitals

Heart rate. Blood pressure. Tracked continuously, without a single wire touching their skin.

Medical-grade biometric sensing built into a single, silent device. No cuffs to put on. No straps to remember. Just a steady picture of how they're doing — minute by minute.

02 — Location

Know they made it home. Know they're still home. Know the moment something changes.

Real-time presence and movement, shown in your app the way you'd want to see it — calm, clear, and only when it matters. No surveillance. Just the answer to "are they okay?"

03 — Alerts

If something looks wrong, your phone knows before they do.

Vital sign anomalies, falls, prolonged inactivity — surfaced gently, instantly, and with the context you need to act. Quiet when life is normal. Decisive when it isn't.

The technology

Built on contactless mmWave radar.

The same medical-grade sensing technology used in modern automotive safety systems, refined for the home. SeniorConnex is powered by Pontosense — a leader in contactless biometric radar — and detects micromovements as small as two-tenths of a millimeter from across the room.

No cameras. No microphones. No wearables. Just a quiet signal in the room, listening for the rhythms of a life well-lived.
