What SeniorConnex does.

SeniorConnex securely relays real-time biometrics to caregivers and family, captured seamlessly via wireless mmWave radar and wearable EKG bands.

If a sudden change or fall is detected, our system triggers an instant SMS alert to ensure immediate action.
