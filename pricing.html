Plain-English breakdown

What you actually pay for, and why.

Hardware is real hardware — Samsung devices, a Movesense chest strap, a radar sensor. We don't mark it up like a medical device because most of what you're paying for isn't proprietary; it's the integration.

Software pays for the alert pipeline that doesn't drop, the portal your caregivers actually use, and a backend that's careful with personal health data. At pilot scale, the recurring number is small — Twilio and Atlas are the line items that scale with you.
