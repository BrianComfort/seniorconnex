The challenge

Modern care runs on thin margins and impossible workloads.

Staffing shortages, alarm fatigue, and rising acuity have made it nearly impossible to know what's happening in every room at every moment. Families want more visibility. Regulators want better outcomes. Your staff want time back. The old playbook — manual rounds, button-press pendants, and clipboard charting — wasn't designed for this.

01

Staffing shortages

Nightly rounds are stretched thin. Critical moments can slip between checks, and incidents are too often discovered after the fact.

02

Wearables don't stick

Pendants get removed. Wristbands need charging. Compliance drops within weeks of admission — the very residents who need monitoring most are the ones who stop wearing it.

03

Family communication gaps

Adult children call the front desk for updates. Staff stop what they're doing to give them. Nobody walks away feeling fully informed.

The solution

Continuous coverage. Zero staff effort.

A small, ceiling- or wall-mounted mmWave radar quietly observes the room — heart rate, respiration, presence, and movement — through clothes, blankets, and walls. There's nothing for the resident to wear, charge, or remember. Nothing for staff to manage. Just a calm baseline of "everything's fine" punctuated by instant escalation when it isn't.

  • Contactless biometric monitoring across every room
  • Works through sheets and clothing — even in the dark
  • Wearable EKG band available for residents who need cardiac-grade tracking
  • No app for staff to babysit, no batteries to replace

One pane of glass

A single dashboard for the clinical team. A private live view for each family.

Clinicians see the whole floor at a glance — vitals, presence, and alert status, room by room. Families see only their loved one — a calm, glanceable view designed to reduce calls to the front desk and increase confidence in your care.

For your clinical team

  • Floor-wide vitals and presence in a single view
  • Audit-ready trend history for every resident
  • Role-based access for admins, nurses, and care aides
  • HIPAA-compliant, AES-256 encrypted end-to-end

For each family

  • A private, mobile-friendly view of their loved one
  • Live vitals and presence — no daily call required
  • Immediate SMS the moment something changes
  • Permissions managed by your team, revocable at any time

Instant escalation

The right person, on the phone, in seconds.

When vitals drift outside a resident's baseline, when a fall is detected, or when movement stops where it shouldn't — SeniorConnex routes an SMS alert to the on-shift staff first and, on your policy, to the designated family contact. No proprietary pager. No paid app. Just a text on the phone that's already in their pocket.

  • Fall detection without floor sensors or cameras
  • Escalation rules per shift, per resident, per situation
  • Two-way acknowledgment so nothing falls through the cracks
