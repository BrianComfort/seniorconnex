What we sell

A complete care kit and the software that turns it into one network.

SeniorConnex is not just a wearable. It is a complete kit: biometric watch, contactless radar sensor, privacy-first in-room camera, and a bedside tablet. All software is HIPAA-compliant and ties the family, the in-home caregiver, and the facility into one shared view and alert system.

Biometric watch

Continuous heart rate, rest, motion, and fall detection without being intrusive.

Pontosense radar

Wall-mounted mmWave sensor measures position and breathing without a camera in rooms where you want privacy, such as a bedroom.

Room camera

A camera to capture an entire room.

Bedside tablet

One-tap video calls. Big buttons, large type, no menus to navigate. Designed for the person being cared for.

The process

From the first conversation to the first quiet night.

We walk every family through the same five steps. Click any step to read what happens.

A 20-minute call with a real person

We map the care network — who needs to be in, who gets alerted first.

 +
You tell us who's in the family, whether there's an in-home caregiver, and whether your loved one lives independently or in a facility. We confirm the escalation policy: who gets the SMS first, who hears it second, and who only sees it on the dashboard. No sales pitch, no demo theater.

The kit arrives pre-provisioned

Everything is already linked to your care network — no setup wizards.

 +
One box. Inside: the watch, the radar sensor, the privacy-shutter camera, the tablet, and a one-page quick-start. Each piece is already paired to your care network. Plug in the tablet, stick the radar to the wall, slip the watch on. That's the install.

Your first reading in under five minutes

The watch starts streaming. The radar calibrates. The network goes live.

 +
The watch streams its first heart rate within a minute. The radar reads the empty-room baseline within three. Everyone in the care network gets a welcome notification and sees the live feed on their phone.

Alerts go live on day one

Falls and vitals changes route by the policy you set.

 +
A real fall escalates by SMS in seconds — to the caregiver on shift first, to family on the policy you set. Two-way acknowledgment confirms the message landed with someone who can act. The system documents every alert and acknowledgment automatically.

We check in. You stay connected.

Weekly for the first month, monthly thereafter. Always a human.

 +
For the first month, a real person calls weekly to tune thresholds, adjust escalation, and make sure the watch sits comfortably. After that, monthly check-ins. The line is never automated — same person every time.

A glanceable view

Open the app. Feel reassured.

The first thing you see is the answer. Heart rate, rest, presence — clear and in plain language. The same truth the facility sees, the same truth the in-home caregiver sees. No second-hand summaries, no "let me check and call you back."

  • Live vitals from the biometric watch, refreshed continuously
  • One-tap video call from the tablet — no buttons for them to learn
  • Privacy-based — no cameras pointed at the bed, no audio in the room
  • SMS alert the moment a real fall is detected
